TOK Essay Feedback: What Good Feedback Should Actually Catch

Useful TOK feedback should not only say that a paragraph is weak. It should explain whether the problem is title focus, example choice, missing counterclaim, shallow AOK analysis, or lack of evaluation.

Fast first pass: paste one paragraph into the GPT and ask for examiner-style feedback with revision moves.

Get TOK essay feedback

Ask feedback to check these five things

  1. Does the paragraph answer the prescribed title?
  2. Is the knowledge claim clear?
  3. Does the example support the claim, or just illustrate the topic?
  4. Is there a meaningful counterclaim or limitation?
  5. Does the paragraph end with an implication about knowledge?

Best prompt to use

Paste your paragraph and ask:

Diagnose this TOK paragraph. Tell me whether it is descriptive, analytical, or evaluative. Then identify the weakest sentence and give three revision moves without rewriting the whole essay for me.
