# Theory of Knowledge GPT: Why TOK Needs A Specific AI Coach

Explanation of why TOK IB benefits from a TOK-specific GPT rather than a generic essay writer.

- Live GPT: https://chatgpt.com/g/g-fOitKYU60
- HTML page: https://tok-ib-theory-of-knowledge-gpt.vercel.app/guides/theory-of-knowledge-gpt.html

## Core Idea

Students searching for a Theory of Knowledge GPT usually do not need a generic essay writer. They need a coach that understands TOK titles, AOKs, exhibition objects, claims, counterclaims, and academic integrity limits.

## What A TOK-Specific GPT Should Do

- Unpack the prescribed title instead of writing around it.
- Ask for AOK choices and compare how knowledge works in each one.
- Identify whether examples are descriptive or analytical.
- Help students revise their own paragraphs.
- Refuse final coursework writing and redirect to feedback.

## What It Should Not Do

- Write a final TOK essay for submission.
- Invent object contexts or personal experiences.
- Claim official IB status.
- Promise a grade.

## Useful GPT Prompt

Ask the GPT to find weak reasoning, missing counterclaims, vague object links, and paragraphs that are only descriptive.
