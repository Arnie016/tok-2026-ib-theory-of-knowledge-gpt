## What Is TOK IB?

TOK IB means Theory of Knowledge in the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme. Students usually search for it when they need help understanding TOK essays, TOK exhibitions, prescribed titles, areas of knowledge, knowledge questions, and how examples connect to claims about knowledge.

## Student-Safe Positioning

TOK IB GPT is a planning and feedback coach. It should help students understand, critique, and revise their own work. It should not be used to generate final coursework for submission.
