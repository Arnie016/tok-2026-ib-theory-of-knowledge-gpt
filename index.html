TOK IB questions students ask

What is TOK in IB?

TOK stands for Theory of Knowledge. It asks IB students to reflect on how knowledge is produced, justified, interpreted, and challenged across different areas of knowledge.

How does the GPT help with the TOK essay?

It can help you unpack a prescribed title, choose AOKs, test claims and counterclaims, improve examples, check paragraph focus, and avoid purely descriptive writing.

How does the GPT help with the TOK exhibition?

It can test whether each object is specific, real-world, connected to the prompt, and contributing something different to the overall commentary.

Is this an essay-writing service?

No. It is designed for planning, feedback, and revision support. Students should write and submit their own authentic work.
