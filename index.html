Why this is different from generic ChatGPT

Generic AI can produce fluent text, but TOK needs a specific kind of thinking: title focus, knowledge questions, areas of knowledge, counterclaims, implications, and evaluation. This GPT is configured to coach those moves rather than write a final submission.

Academic integrity first

The GPT is positioned as a planning and feedback tool. It should not write final essays or final exhibition commentaries for submission. Students remain responsible for authentic work and school academic integrity rules.
